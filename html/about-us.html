About us

We're a group of folks who are dedicated to spreading the word about yoga's healing properties.
We've seen the difference yoga can make in our own lives,
and we believe that if people just learned to take better care of their bodies, they could save a lot of misery and sickness.
Yoga has a lot to give; it not only makes you fit,
but it also gradually alters your mind and body by developing greater integration and depth at all levels of our being.
