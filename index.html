What makes our yoga classes so special?

We're a group of people who are passionate about spreading the word about yoga's
therapeutic benefits. We've witnessed the positive effects of yoga in our own lives, and we feel that if people just
learned to take better care of their bodies,
they could save a lot of suffering and illness.
Yoga has a lot to offer; it not only helps you become in shape,
but it also helps you relax. However, it gradually changes your mind and body by increasing
integration and depth at all levels of our being.
